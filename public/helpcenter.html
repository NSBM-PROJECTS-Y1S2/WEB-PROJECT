MEDIX HELP CENTER

Welcome to the Medix Help Center. Here you will find answers to common questions and guidance on how to make the most of our platform.

Getting Started

How to Register as a Customer

  1. Click on the "Account" button on the top right corner of the page.
  2. If you are a buyer click on "Customer Account" button.
  3. Click on "Create an Account" button on bottom of the form.
  4. Fill in the required details and click "Sign Up".
  5. Verify your email address by clicking on the link sent to your email.

How to Register as a Pharmacy

  1. Click on the "Account" button on the top right corner of the page.
  2. If you are a seller click on "Pharmacy Account" button.
  3. Click on "Create an Account" button on bottom of the form.
  4. Fill in the required details and click "Sign Up".
  5. Our team will review your application and contact you within 1-3 business days.

Managing Your Account

How to Update Your Profile

  1. Click on the "Account" button on the top right corner of the page.
  2. Log in to your Medix account.
  3. Go to "My Account" and select "Profile Settings".
  4. Update your details and click "Save".

How to Change Your Password

  1. Click on the "Account" button on the top right corner of the page.
  2. Log in to your Medix account.
  3. Go to "My Account" and select "Account Settings" then select "Change Password".
  4. Enter your current password and new password, then click "Save".

Placing Orders

How to Search for Products

  1. Use the search bar at the top of the page to search for products by name or category.
  2. Click on the product you want to view more details.
  3. Click "Add to Cart" to add the product to your shopping cart.

How to Place an Order

  1. Go to your shopping cart and review the items.
  2. Click "Proceed to Checkout" and enter your delivery address.
  3. Select your payment method and click "Proceed".
  4. Review your order and click "Confirm Order".

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I reset my password?

  1. Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page.
  2. Enter your registered email address and click "Submit."
  3. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

What should I do if I receive a damaged item?

  1. Contact our customer support team within 24 hours of receiving the item.
  2. Provide photos of the damaged item and packaging.
  3. Our team will review your request and provide a replacement or refund.

How do I update my pharmacy information?

  1. Log in to your pharmacy account on Medix.
  2. Go to "Seller Dashboard" and select "Account Settings".
  3. Make the necessary changes and click "Save".

Payments and Billing

Accepted Payment Methods

  1. We accept Visa, MasterCard, and other major credit/debit cards.
  2. You can also pay with PayPal or bank transfer.
  3. Cash On Delivery.

How to View Your Order History

  1. Click on the "Account" button on the top right corner of the page.
  2. Log in to your Medix account.
  3. Go to "My Account" and select "My Orders".
  4. View your past orders and details.

Shipping and Delivery

Shipping Options

  1. Standard Shipping (5-7 business days)
  2. Express Shipping (2-3 business days)
  3. Overnight Shipping (1 business day)

How to Track Your Order

  1. Click on the "Account" button on the top right corner of the page.
  2. Log in to your Medix account.
  3. Go to "My Account" and select "My Orders".
  4. Click "Track Order" to view the current status and estimated delivery date.

Returns and Refunds

How to Return an Item

  1. Go to "My Account" and select "My Orders".
  2. Click "Return Item" next to the order you want to return.
  3. Fill in the return form and submit your request.
  4. Our team will review your request and contact you within 1-3 business days.

Refund Policy

  1. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products.
  2. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund.
  3. Refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item.

Selling on Medix

How to List Products

  1. Click on the "Account" button on the top right corner of the page.
  2. Log in to your pharmacy account on Medix.
  3. Go to "Seller Dashboard" and select "Add New Product".
  4. Enter product details, including name, description, price, and quantity.
  5. Upload product images and click "Save".

How to Manage Orders

  1. Go to "Seller Dashboard" and select "Orders".
  2. View your pending orders and click "Accept" or "Reject".
  3. View new orders and update their status as they are processed and shipped.

For further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact our support team. We are here to help you with any questions or concerns you may have.
